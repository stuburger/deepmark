Q002
Analyse the impact of expanding using a franchising model for the home decorating service rather than the company employing its own additional staff and running the service themselves. [12 marks]
6/12
Override mark
max 12
If Jim were to choose to become a franchisor, it would take time and effort but he would have advantages in the long run. If he chose then the orders that he is receiving from the far locations can also buy wallpaper. This is because the franchisees could locate themselves near to the orders. This leads to profits increasing and to which Jim could supply more wallpaper. However, because opening a franchise is expanding organically ✓ this means that Jim might not receive higher profits straight away. This also means that he would have to wait a long time for enough franchises to be opened so that further away customers can buy wallpaper. Profits not increasing quickly leads to Jim not having enough money to pay for paint machinery. Since the demand for paint in his company is high, he would have to make it in large quantities, which he doesn't have money for, and he might also loose some potential customers, therefore further decreasing his profits.

If Jim choose to recruit managers and decorators for an in-home decorating service then he would not have to worry about the amount of labour nearby. This is because in his area there is a rising employment levels meaning that more people could come to work as a manager or decorator. This would increase customer loyalty as people are thankful that Jim's company provides this additional offer.
